Plaza Mayor de Fusagasugá: Centro histórico y punto de encuentro cultural, rodeado de arquitectura republicana y la imponente Iglesia de Nuestra Señora de Belén.

Main Square of Fusagasugá: Historical center and cultural meeting point, surrounded by Republican architecture and the imposing Church of Our Lady of Belén.

Casa de la Cultura: Una casona antigua que alberga museos arqueológicos con piezas de la cultura Panche y salones dedicados al arte contemporáneo regional.

House of Culture: An old mansion that houses archaeological museums with pieces from the Panche culture and rooms dedicated to regional contemporary art.
